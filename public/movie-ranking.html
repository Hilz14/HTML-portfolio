The Three Best Movies According to Hilary

Introduction


Movies present a unique of opportunity to escape from the trials and tribulations of a mundane existence. Below, you will find listed the top movies which will help one achieve the goal of true escapism. Enjoy.

1. Shawshank Redemption

Shawshank redemption is a brilliant, severely underestimated cinematic work of art by the one and only Frank Darabont. this movie explores many complex themes and overall is a movie showcasing the power of strength and resilience by an ordinary individual.

2. The Truman show

Jim Carey. Just when you think you know an Actor,they show you a whole new dimension. I am astounded at the lack of awards received by Carey himself for the portrayal of Truman, a man who had the rights to his slife sold by his parents to a television corporation and in turn had his whole life broadcasted to the whole world. Without his knowledge of course. And only till he found out. I promise, that is not a spoiler in the least. Bravo sir, they just don't make movies like that any more.

3. Shutter Island

At which point are we going to retrospectively give Leonardo DiCaprio the awards he deserves for his tremendous acting skills. This film messed with my mind so bad, and trust me I thought inception was bad.
Run, don't walk to watch this. You will not regret it .
